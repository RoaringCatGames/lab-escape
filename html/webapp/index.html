Lab Escape

An infinite runner where you're running to escape a laboratory. You are running as fast as you can, and more and more obstacles are appearing in your path. You must engage the appropriate force fields to break through the walls. If you engage the wrong force field, you will crash into the wall, and your escape is over.

controls:

  • Engage Forcefields:
    • Lightning (blue) - A
    • Laser (yellow)   - S
    • Plasma (green)   - D
    • Or tap the buttons below the player.
  • Restart On Death - Space
